About Me

I am Pablo, a man of many talents. Media production, marketing, and coding are among my most notable skills. Based out of Chicago, IL, I have a Kanye West attitude towards innovative projects minus the drama.

People who know me would say, I love coding, photos and soccer, (not in that order). If you want to get in touch, please know I prefer texts/e-mails over calls, otherwise phone's on DND!

I would like to thank my instructor Ramzi Bach for creating Slaying the Dragon and inspiring me to become the web developer I am destined to be. The goal is to combine my interests and skills to start a project of my own.
